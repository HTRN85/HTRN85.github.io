Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2024

Your Privacy Matters

At HTRN85 DNS Security, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information
  • Company name
  • Contact name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
Payment Information
  • Processed securely by Square (PCI DSS compliant)
  • We do not store credit card numbers
  • We only store transaction IDs and amounts
Usage Information
  • DNS query logs (domains, timestamps)
  • Client computer names
  • IP addresses (for service delivery)
  • Client installation data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide DNS security services
  • Billing: To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Support: To respond to customer inquiries
  • Security: To detect and prevent threats
  • Improvements: To enhance our services

We will never: Sell your data, share without consent, or use for advertising

3. Data Security

Security Measures
  • ✅ Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • ✅ Encryption at Rest: Database encryption enabled
  • ✅ Access Controls: Role-based access to data
  • ✅ Audit Logging: All access logged and monitored
  • ✅ Rate Limiting: Protection against abuse
  • ✅ Input Validation: All input sanitized and validated
  • ✅ Secure Coding: OWASP best practices followed

4. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while subscription is active + 90 days
  • DNS Logs: Retained for 90 days (for security analysis)
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Deleted Accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

  • ✅ Access: Request a copy of your data
  • ✅ Correction: Update incorrect information
  • ✅ Deletion: Request account and data deletion
  • ✅ Portability: Export your data
  • ✅ Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails

To exercise these rights, email: privacy@htrn85dns.com
